Products

We offer an immense variety of sweet treats from across the globe, ranging from chocolate truffles to fruit jellies. Regardless of the season, you're sure to find all of your favourite scrumptious delicacies lining our shelves. Our all time best sellers are featured below.

Candy Apples

Beau's original recipe was developed in 1908 on a chilly fall evening. These apples are covered in a crunchy toffee cinnamon coating. Special care is taken to make sure they deliver a powerful tangy flavour.

Lolipops

Ottawa's finest selection of this crowd pleasing candy on a stick. The psychedelic swirling colours will have you hypnotized for days. But you just can't get enough! Make sure you buy in bulk for extra savings.

Jube Jubes

Sticky, colourful gummies that will enlighten your taste-buds to new worlds of possibility. Our delicious jube jubes are produced fresh daily in small batches by our dedicated candy chefs. Beau's bets you can't have just one!

Hours

Beau's BonBons is proud to be open seven days a week: 10am to 8pm on weekdays and 11am to 4pm on weekends.

Location

Beau's BonBons opened up shop at 137 Dalhousie St. over one hundred years ago. It is centrally located in the heart of Ottawa's historic Byward Market at the intersection of Dalhousie and Murray St. There is free parking located behind the building. Please note that our shop entrance is wheelchair accessible.
